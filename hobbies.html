I've been going to folklore classes for 9 years. Through the years I have made some great friendships and enjoyed many trips with the group, in the country and abroad. We've visited Turkey, Hungary, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia, Serbia, Bulgaria through festivals where we met with teenagers our age from around the whole world. On every festival there are activities that bring us closer to each other while learning something new about each country.

I started exercising daily about a year ago and I love it. It gives me more energy throughout the whole day.

When I have some free time I enjoy watching TV-shows.

Music has always been very important to me. I spend most of my day with headphones on. It helps me relax and it makes studying way more interesting. Recently I even started learning how to play the guitar.
