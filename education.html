Faculty

Currently, I am ending my first year as a student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje. Till now I gained a lot of knowledge from the classes I was taking, so I will be anticipating the following years at the faculty.

High School

I studied high school at "Josif Josifovski" Gevgelija where I had the opportunity to learn from great professors. Throughout the four years I participated in a lot of competitions and provided myself with additional knowledge apart from the school books. Some of my most honorable awards and certificates were:

  • Award for the best student in High School "Josif Josifovski" Gevgelija in the generation 2018/22
  • First prize at the State Biology Competition 2021
  • Gold Honour at the International Mathematics Challenge IYMC 2019 and placement among the top 5% of all participants from around the world
  • Gold and Silver medal at the OFMA 2021 and OFMA 2022 International Olympiads in Financial Mathematics and Actuarial Science
  • Gold medal and first place at the International Mathematical Competition Kangaroo Final Round 2019
  • First places in the State Mathematics Competition PI-DEN 2021 and 2022
  • Third prize at the State Competition in Biology 2019
  • Eight place in the State Chemistry Competition 2019 and placement in the Macedonian Olympiad in Chemistry
  • Numerous awards and certificates on municipal, regional and national level of competitions in Mathematics, Physics, Biology and Chemistry

Primary School

In 2009 I started primary school at "Vlado Kantardziev" Gevgelija. From the very beginning I loved Math, so I started going to competitions from fourth grade and never missed a year. Later I showed interest in Biology, Chemistry and Physics, so by the time I finished primary school I had achieved great results in every field I liked:

  • Second prize at the State Physics Competition 2018
  • Second prize at the State Chemistry Competition 2018
  • Participation in the Macedonian Olympiad in Natural Sciences 2018
  • Third prize at the State Biology Competition 2017
  • Silver medal at the International Mathematical Competition Kangaroo Final Round 2018
  • Regular participation and honorable mentions in the State Mathematics Competitions
